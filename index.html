A gentle daily companion

Daily Anchor helps you get through the basics, one honest day at a time

Daily Anchor is a simple daily task tracker built for the days when even brushing your teeth or eating something feels like a lot. No shame, no streaks-shaming, no perfectionism - just small tasks, honest points, and steady encouragement.

Start using Daily Anchor See how it works

What it does

  • Tracks everyday tasks - hygiene, eating, chores, and more - worth points that add up to 100 a day
  • Matches your task list to goals you choose: weight loss, keto, mental health, fitness, sleep, or your own
  • Logs meals, carbs, and protein with a simple tap counter
  • Sends medication reminders on your own schedule, only if you want them
  • Tracks your daily score, weekly average, and streaks over time
  • Unlocks achievement badges for consistency, not perfection

Frequently asked questions

Is Daily Anchor free?
Yes, Daily Anchor is free to use in the browser right now.

Do I need to create an account?
No. Your data stays in your own browser - no sign-up required.

Is this a substitute for medical or mental health care?
No. Daily Anchor is a self-guided motivation tool, not medical, psychological, or nutritional advice. Please see a qualified healthcare provider for guidance specific to you.

Is there an iPhone app?
An iOS app is in progress. In the meantime, you can add this website to your iPhone home screen from Safari's Share menu for an app-like experience.
